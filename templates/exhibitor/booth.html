In the last years, we often had the problem that the exhibition area in the mensa was overcrowded. Thus, we decided that booths in the mensa area can only have either normal tables or bartables. Please state your preferred location if you have one. In the case that everyone fits in the mensa area, we will not offer booths in the first floor.
